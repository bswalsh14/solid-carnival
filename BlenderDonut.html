Donut Project

Used this youtube series as a tutorial. Following step-by-step first created a table, then a donut, then a coffee mug. After that, added the frosting and sprinkles particles. The frosting colors and design went away when I added sprinkles particles. Asked Reddit and was told to in the emissions tab activate "use modifiers stack". Then in the Render tab clicked "emitter". This fixed it and both modifiers are active.

  • Created the table with a plane, used an image. Shift-A then image texture to add image.
  • Created the mug, used a picture as a background image. Render, hit N, check background images and hit add image.
  • Created the donut, then hit shift D to create copy to create the frosting and scaled it to make it larger. Hit O to do random variation for frosting.
  • Used node editor, with gloss and diffuse and mix shader for textures.
  • Created two sprinkles, one big one small. Went to particles, adjusted size and added those to the donut.

Worldbuilding Project

Putting all my worldbuilding resources here before I actually get into it. Reddit's getting started guide https://www.reddit.com/r/worldbuilding/wiki/getting_started .


Premise. Short paragraph, 3-4 lines that outlines world. Example, Heavenly Task Force Mega Ultra Mk. I: See No Evil is a space fantasy project that is supposed to be a parody of superhero, sci-fi and fantasy genres. It takes tropes from anime, video games, films and books and mashes them up in a colorful and lighthearted universe full of fluff. The project focuses on the adventures of two airheads known as the "Heavenly Task Force Mega Ultra Mk. I: See No Evil", hence the name of the project.
